Beef Tacos

Description

This recipe will show you how to make one of my favorite quickk dinners! Beef Tacos!

Ingredients

  • 1 Packet - Of soft or hard flour tortillas
  • 1 - 1Lbs Ground Beef
  • 1 - Taco seasoning packet
  • 1/3 Cup - Water
  • Optional - Your choice of toppings

Steps

  1. Warm up a greased skillet on the stove on Med - High heat
  2. Place ground beef in the skillet.
  3. Slice and dice your grond beef in the pan while cooking.
  4. Brown the meat.
  5. Pour in your 3/4 cup of water.
  6. Immediately after pouring water pour in your taco seasoning.
  7. Simmer your meat with the taco seasoning. Stire to mix evenly.
  8. Meat is done when the taco sauce is thick.
  9. Optional- Add additional toppings as you see fit ie. cheese,lettuce, tomato, etc.
  10. Place meat in your tortilla shells and or toppings.
  11. Enjoy!
