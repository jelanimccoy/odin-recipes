Peanut Butter & Jelly

Description

This recipe will show you how to make one of my favorite quickk lunches! A Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwhich. I hope you enjoy it for your lunches just as much as I do!

Ingredients

  • 2 - Slices of Bread (Your choice on type)
  • 1 - Butter knife full of Peanut Butter
  • 1 - Butter knife full of Jelly (Your favorite flavor ofcourse!)

Steps

  1. have both of your slices of bread side by side face up.
  2. spread your butter knife full of peanut butter evenly across your first slice of bread (On the side facing you.).
  3. Wipe the residual peanut butter off on the other slice of bread.
  4. Open the jelly and take a butter knife full of the jelly.
  5. Spread the jelly evenly across the entire side of the piece of bread.
  6. Take the peanut butter covered bread slice and place it on top of the jelly slice (Condiment side facing each other).
  7. Press down with gentle pressure.
  8. Optional- Cut into half
  9. Enjoy!
